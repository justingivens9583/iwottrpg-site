Sir Anders Skysprint

Campaign: IWO (Original)

Basic Info

  • Race:Human
  • Class:Paladin Oath of Devotion

Backstory

A noble and honorable Paladin of Bahamut. He was the holder of the powerful hammer Storm's Herald. He was known for his generosity and his faithfulness to his friends. He was one of the founders of the revived Independent Wanderers Organization. Some of his exploits were the clearing of the Everforge of Kobolds, the defense of Skalington, the cleansing of the Bronze Shrine and saving the dragon Lhammaruntosz, and the battle of Everforge to end the Dwarven Inquisition.
