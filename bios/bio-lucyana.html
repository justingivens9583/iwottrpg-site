Character Bio

Lucyana Orilith

ClassWarlock
RaceTiefling

Backstory

Talented leather worker and is just all around a nice person. She has made quite a living for herself with her quality work and friendly demeanor. She met Idris and fell hard without realizing he was a deity. He had never been with a mortal and was quite surprised to find out how very fragile they are, and it stresses him out.

He imbibes her with magic and gives her a ring that is his secret amulet that enables him to enter a pact linking them. She knows, but lets him think it is still a secret because she thinks it is cute.
