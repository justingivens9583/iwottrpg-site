Character Bio

Squeak

ClassArtificer
RaceHalfling

Backstory

A mad scientist and doctor of sorts. Squeak is giggly, maniacal, and delightfully unpredictable. She sometimes speaks in threes — yes yes yes, no no no, silly silly silly. She has a particular fondness for bones, keeping the best as trophies, with the hyoid bone holding a special place in her collection.

Chaotic neutral through and through — she doesn't really care if something is right or wrong, as long as she gets a bone out of it. Her mind wanders and she sometimes doesn't listen when she should. Strangely enough, she came from a perfectly happy middle-class family with loving parents. She's just off her rocker, and nobody quite knows why.
